# HCJK Collection - Content Strategy & Rotation Schedule

## üìä Format Mix Strategy (70/20/10)

### 70% iPad Handwritten Format - Primary Identity
**When**: Monday, Wednesday, Friday every week  
**Why**: Maintains authentic voice and brand recognition  
**Style**: Your natural handwriting aesthetic with iPad formatting  

**Best For**:
- Personal reflections
- Emotional poems
- Intimate themes
- Daily moments
- Relationship explorations

**Production Notes**:
- Write directly on iPad as usual
- Export as high-quality images
- Maintain consistent background/lighting
- Include natural imperfections for authenticity

---

### 20% Typed Minimalist Format - Professional Polish
**When**: Every other Friday (alternating with visual poetry)  
**Why**: Accommodates longer poems and professional presentation  
**Style**: Clean typography with minimalist card design  

**Best For**:
- Poems over 20 lines
- Complex structures
- Submission-ready pieces
- Mental health collection (due to sensitive nature)
- Formal/academic themes

**Design Template**:
```
White card background
Centered typography
Crimson Text font for body
Dancing Script for titles
Subtle shadow effects
```

---

### 10% Visual Poetry Format - Special Engagement
**When**: Monthly special feature (last Friday of month)  
**Why**: Creates shareable content for social media engagement  
**Style**: Poem overlaid on photography or artistic backgrounds  

**Best For**:
- Seasonal poems
- Nature themes
- Special occasions
- Marketing/promotional content
- Instagram/visual platforms

**Visual Style**:
- Soft focus backgrounds
- Text overlay with good contrast
- Instagram-ready dimensions
- Consistent color palette with brand

## üîß Technical Setup

### iPad Handwritten Production
- **Apps**: Procreate, Notability, or GoodNotes
- **Export**: PNG high-resolution (300 DPI)
- **Backup**: Cloud storage + local copy
- **Consistency**: Use same paper background/font settings

### Typed Minimalist Production
- **Tools**: Canva Pro, Figma, or Adobe InDesign
- **Templates**: Pre-designed cards with brand colors
- **Fonts**: Great Vibes (titles), Crimson Text (body)
- **Export**: Multiple formats (PNG, PDF, social media sizes)

### Visual Poetry Production
- **Photography**: Phone camera + editing apps
- **Design**: Over, Canva, or Adobe Express
- **Dimensions**: Instagram square (1080x1080), stories (1080x1920)
- **Text**: High contrast, readable fonts, consistent sizing
